Let me answer a few of the common questions here.

Yes, you have to use the Slideshow application.
Please remember that we are looking for links to slideshows. We won't accept single person entries in this thread. Thank you all for the excitement for this. I'm excited as well.
Tutorial - I'm sorry that it was down but it's back up and complete - You can find the video here - http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sGEIcRnV1Hc
Please, post your finished slideshow links here in this thread. Do not post anything else. I will remove wishlists, off topic conversation or spam.
Please do include a story. This is a storytelling contest with images of your habitats. So, make it a story. You have plenty of time to enter, don't feel rushed.
Unless your Club has less than 10 people, please try not to use one person's habitat more than once. This is a contest designed to let all members of your club participate. Work together as a team and you will be overjoyed with the results.
Team Leaders do not have to be Club Founders, or even Officers. As long as the Club members agree on who is going to build the final presentation, it can be anyone in the Club that has the desire to help and will be responsible for getting the final show link here in this thread.
Multiple Scrapbooks - Go to your team-mates scrapbook and choose the image. Right Click on the Image and choose Copy Image Location. I simply used mine in the tutorial because I had it set up with another image.

For now, here is a written guide to making a slideshow.
How to - Getting the Image:
1. Take a snapshot of your habitat.
2. From the Scrapbook, click on the image that you want to put into the slideshow.
3. Right click on the image and select “Copy Image Location”

Creating the Slideshow:
1. Go to www.slide.com . If you haven’t, create an account.
2. Click on The Slideshow Image
3. Click on Add Image From – Image URL
4. Insert the link that you got from step 3 of Getting the Image
5. Click “Get My Image”
Repeat steps 4 and 5 until all of the images are loaded into your Slide Show.
6. Get the link to paste on the forums.
