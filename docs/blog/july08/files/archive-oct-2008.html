All Vampirates Books Getting Graphic Revamp

Wednesday, 29 October 2008 Filed in: Books
According to thebookseller.com the Vampirates series of books is getting a graphic revamp with a new graphic novel style cover. These are being produced by comic book artist Kev Walker of Judge Dredd and the Young Bond fame.
