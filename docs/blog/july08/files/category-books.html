Covers and Bob Lea

Sunday, 16 November 2008
According to Justin Somper on the official website "Firstly, I don't have a whole lot of say in the jacket look - as those of you who read this blog regularly will know. Secondly, the fact that the books are getting rejacketed is in no way a thumbs down for the marvelous Bob Lea or his work. All popular book sequences get freshened up from time to time. When BLACK HEART comes out in 2009, it will have been a full four years since DEMONS first swam into view. So I think everyone felt it was a good time to run with something a bit different."
