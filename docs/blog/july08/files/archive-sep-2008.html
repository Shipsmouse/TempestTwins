Date Change

Thursday, 25 September 2008 Filed in: Justin Somper
From the Nocturn
"BLACK HEART, the fourth book in the VAMPIRATE series by Justin Somper has had its publication post-poned by two months. The book which was scheduled for publication on 5th January will now have it's general release on 2nd March 2009. Here is what Justin had to say on the matter when I asked him to confirm or deny the publication date change:
"Yes, BLACK HEART has moved to March. It's hard telling my loyal readers that they have to wait a bit longer - especially as there's been a bit of a gap since BLOOD CAPTAIN - but I hope they'll understand that it's all in order to deliver a better book to them, which is really the most important thing to me."
 
By the time BLACK HEART is released, fans will have been waiting a year and a half to get their hands on this next installment, but I personally feel that if Justin needs more time to deliver a book he is proud of then it doesn't matter. BLOOD CAPTAIN was amazing and this series keeps going from strength to strength.
 
This date change will most likely mean that all foreign dates mentioned so far are going to change. Most likely, just by two months, but it is not yet known."

It Be Talk Like A Pirate Day Today

Arrr, I did find one of me sword earrings going to work in me Hubbie's ear, me had to remove off a certain young pirate a hat and dagger before going into class (his teacher eyed his jolly roger socks suspiciously) and a young wench is getting very confused asking for balloons with pumpkins on to be blown up and going to nursery with her black cat hat on. Me have promised to cook a bat cake for her before changing into my dress. - me be off to the phrases page to make this sound better. I hope me hearties ye are doing better.
