Traditional Breakfast with Justin

Thursday, 02 April 2009
Justin Somper 'is on a book tour of the United States, will greet fans at "Vampirates Breakfast," 10 a.m. Saturday, April 4, at Magic Tree Bookstore, 141 N. Oak Park Ave., Oak Park.

"We are going to have a traditional breakfast with the author," said Magic Tree co-owner Rose Joseph. "We'll have coffee or juice, and probably donuts. However, the treat will be having Justin Somper. He's from the U.K. and he'll be talking about his books, which are unique." She noted that the series is set "sometime in the future although many aspects of them seem very historical.""

My name is (sorry it is our policy here not to print names) and Im actually best friends with Bob Lea. And he based Grace Tempest on me (when he did the 3D render).
