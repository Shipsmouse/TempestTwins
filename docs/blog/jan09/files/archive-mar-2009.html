Bob Lea based Grace Tempest on me

Friday, 06 March 2009 Filed in: Books
You have got to read this message I got sent .....

My name is (sorry it is our policy here not to print names) and Im actually best friends with Bob Lea. And he based Grace Tempest on me (when he did the 3D render). I have Auburn hair (like Grace), Im Australian and I have the same figure and height as Grace (in the book she has green eyes, I have blue..but that doesnt matter and she's tanned, but Justin told Bob to make her pale and Im pale). So apart from the 3 year age gap and the eyes Im Grace Tempest from top to bottom. Id love to play her if they make movies of the series!! Sadly, Bob isnt the illustrator anymore if you dont believe me that I know Bob Ill send you his email address (if you dont have it) and you can ask him yourself. So yeah....he based 3D Grace on me ^_^ cause Im the living incarnate of Grace Tempest lol!!

UK covers

Tuesday, 03 March 2009 Filed in: Books
I've got the vampirate books which don't have the skulls on the front, the other ones, such as on demons of the ocean its the vampirate ship on the front instead. Would you be able to tell me when the next book (Black Heart) with the same style of covers are out? Are they the US covers? And have you got the picture of the Black Heart cover in that series of book covers?

All the UK covers are been revamped to be skulls with windows through the eyes. The US covers are still by their usual artist.

Bob Lea (who drew the covers of the books you have) is no longer producing the UK covers.
