The Dirty Dolphin Jukebox at the Calle del Marinero


From Dead Deep.

Jez roll his eyes at Connor. "There really aren't enough free yabbies in the world to make me want to listen to this." He reached his hand across the bar and squeezed Bart's shoulder. "Mate, play another record please!"

"Actually," said Connor, "that's not such a bad idea!" He glanced over at the jukebox in the corner of the bar. The old Wurlitzer had been pounding out thrash-shanty ever since they arrived. After a time, the music felt like a severe assault on the eardrums.


What collections and songs would you expect find on the Jukebox? Try to keep it relevant to pirating.
