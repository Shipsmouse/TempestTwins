Sue Clearwater, widow of Charlie's friend Harry, had a chair on his other side; her two children, Leah and Seth, were also there, sitting on the ground like the rest of us." ... "The whole pack was there: Sam with his Emily, Paul, Embry, Quil, and Jared with Kim, the girl he'd imprinted upon." ... "He looked at Old Quil Ateata, who shifted in his chair, straightening his frail shoulders. Billy took a drink from a bottle of water and wiped his forehead.
