Captain Molucco Wrathe's Cabin
"His cabin appeared to be vast, and was crammed with all manner of objects. ... towered over a chest, out of which spilled gold coins and jewels. There were paintings - including one of sunflowers ... propped up against antique chairs. Further inside ... were mirrors, higher than Conner's head, which doubled the expanse of booty. ... Finally, as he peered past a pair of tall Chinese vases, he found Molucco Wrathe, sitting like a sultan of ancient times on a mound of plump silk cushions. Beside him, Scrimshaw (his snake) was uncurling on a bright purple cushion and sliding towards a low table to inspect a plate of honeyed dates."
